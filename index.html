About Us

Background

Industrial security services ltd (ISSL) was established in 1992 to fulfill the need for specialized and professional security services. It has been operational for 20 years.

Industrial security services Ltd is a subsidiary company of Madhvani Group Ltd and ISSL is financially sound with strong client base.

With its head office at Plot 37 Kimaka road, Jinja, ISS Ltd is in a position to offer services covering a wide range of applications and expertise on an international level. ISSL has a branch office in Kampala which coordinates its operations in the Kampala area in addition to satellite offices in, Kakira, Tororo, , Bukwo, Butiaba and Kayunga.

ISS Ltd is one of the founder members of Uganda Private Security Association (UPSA) with the current guard force of over 1300 trained and professional guards and is fully registered and legally Licenced by the appropriate authorities in Uganda.

We Lead By Example

The aim of ISS Ltd is to prevent loss of client’s assets and to ensure safety of personnel. This aim is achieved by vigorously maintaining standards in three key areas namely the Company, the Staff and the Client, which make up the fabric of the organization: These include.

  • The Company
  • Staff
  • The client
  • The company has a constant base for its operations. The company has a clear company structure, adequately insured with suitably qualified staff. ISS Ltd has a reputation based upon its service performance and its Policy to employ the correct people in important positions.
  • The staff of Industrial Security Services Ltd undergo stringent selection prior to recruitment with careful vetting. Comprehensive in-house residential training is given to all staff and followed up with a high level of supervision and further on site training. A clear career path gives the men a visible ladder to climb, and we encourage strong and capable field supervision to enhance performance and encourage promotion through good performance.
  • The client is the reason we exist as a company, and therefore gets a fair “Contract” a good service, value for money with an excellent response by senior staff to comment or criticism by the client, and maintain transparency at all times. All clients must ensure that they are comprehensively insured.

The senior Management team is assisted by Junior Management responsible for the following department, operations, Accounts, Administration, Training, Investigation, sales and marketing.
